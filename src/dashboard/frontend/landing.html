Process

How PropSight works

Four steps from raw data to investment insight.

1
Data Harvesting
Scrapes municipal portals for infrastructure tenders and CLU changes. Aggregates listing density and pricing from real estate portals.
2
Signal Correlation
Weights 7 lead and demand indicators using a configurable model to compute a Growth Velocity Score for each zone.
3
Trend Analysis
Computes pricing velocity and rental yield delta to flag Appreciating and Undervalued zones across micro-markets.
4
Visual Projection
Projects GVS trajectories onto an interactive state map with 24–60 month horizon modeling and confidence levels.
